Seal point house cat

The seal point house cat is for anyone looking for a great companion. The only difference between this cat and a show cat is the color points and minor details that would not be ideal for a show cat. All the fun of a show cat, but not intended for shows.

Split colored show

The split colored show cat is the most sought-after Ragdoll. The beauty and rarity of this kitten make it the highest priced kitten we offer, but will win you many shows if cared for and maintained appropriately. Due to the rarity of this kitten there is a waiting list and refunds for this kitten are available on deposits.

Bicolor house cat

The bicolor Ragdoll makes a great house cat. While not suited for shows because of the color pattern, our bicolor kittens are still beautiful to look at, and hold the same personality as any of our other Ragdolls. These Ragdolls are less sought-after than any of our kittens but still a great companion to any home.

Seal point show cat

If you are looking for a great show cat to win many awards, then the seal point Ragdoll show cat is for you. Our seal point cats are bred from grand champions and will have very similar color points. We breed quality over quantity for show cats. Show cats are more expensive but twice as rewarding.

Mitted show cat

Our mitted show Ragdolls are our second most sought- after kitten. Bred from grand champions with the most awards of all our cats, your kitten is sure to steal the show. There is also a waiting list for these kittens but not nearly as long as the waiting list for the split colored Show cats. There is no refunds for these kittens even though there is a waiting list.

Seal colored show/house cat

The seal colored show/house cat is a crowed favorite. Many people purchase the seal colored cat with the uncertainty of putting the cat in shows or not. If you are uncertain of a show cat or house cat this would be the choice for you.

Pricing

The Common cat (mixed)

While the common cat or a cat that is not purebred, can make a great pet, the difference in personality is clearly visible. Comparing a common cat to a Ragdoll is just not justified in any way. The Ragdoll is a lovable pet that demands attention and caring. Any purebred cat has a noticeable difference than a mixed breed, but the Ragdoll is known for its docility and attention seeking behavior. Our Ragdolls are a perfect fit for any family or home. The price of a common cat or mixed breed is normally in this price range. Many have bad reputations due to aggressive behavior or scratching even the owner. This is not a concern with Ragdolls.

Mixed breed (common cat) $0 - $800

The Show cat (purebred)

The show cat is just that, a cat for show but also a house pet. Any show cat requires lots of love and maintenance. Show cats require special attention and can be very rewarding for the owners willing to put in the time and effort required to win shows. Our show cats are the most expensive we offer because they are purebred from our grand champions. We hand select the best kittens suited for show, and with the required time and effort you are sure to experience many wins at your shows. Show cats are for serious cat show competitors, and well worth the money. They are just beautiful to look at.

Show cat (pure bred) $1800 and up

The House cat (purebred)

The house cat makes a great gift companion. While these are just as beautiful and loving as the show cat, these house pets don’t require nearly as much time and maintenance. We hand select the kittens for house pets based on color pattern and details that would rule out a show cat. These are kittens are also bred from champions but may have an undesirable birthmark or color pattern that would hinder show performance. Still the same lovable beautiful Ragdoll but less expensive. Our house kittens can be used for show but not recommended.

House cat(pure bred) $1500 - $3500
