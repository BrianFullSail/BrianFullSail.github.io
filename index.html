Ragdoll breed

The Ragdoll is docile breed with a floppy calm nature. Known for their distinct blue eyes and silky smooth fur, these cats are simply beautiful to look at. Ragdolls are known to go limp In your arms when holding them and hence the name they have been given. Due to the docility of the breed they should remain indoors because they may not be able to defend themselves. And who would want to lose such a beautiful pet anyway?

Show cat

We breed both show cats and simple house pet cats. Our Decision of which kittens are for show and which are for house pets comes from years of experience and a knowledge of what is needed to win at any show. Our cats have won multiple awards and prizes. Having a grand champion as a show cat is a rewarding but demanding experience. House pets are simple, fun and just as rewarding without the demand for so much work.

House cat

Our house pet cats, are bred from the same champions that have won many shows. The difference is only the minor details not suited for show cats. A simple birthmark or color pattern and even size is what dictates our decision. our house cats are free birth defects and will always be the same quality as our show cats minus the show aspect. You can certainly show our house cats but it is not recommended for success.

Pricing

The Common cat (mixed)

While the common cat or a cat that is not purebred, can make a great pet, the difference in personality is clearly visible. Comparing a common cat to a Ragdoll is just not justified in any way. The Ragdoll is a lovable pet that demands attention and caring. Any purebred cat has a noticeable difference than a mixed breed, but the Ragdoll is known for its docility and attention seeking behavior. Our Ragdolls are a perfect fit for any family or home. The price of a common cat or mixed breed is normally in this price range. Many have bad reputations due to aggressive behavior or scratching even the owner. This is not a concern with Ragdolls.

Mixed breed (common cat) $0 - $800

The Show cat (purebred)

The show cat is just that, a cat for show but also a house pet. Any show cat requires lots of love and maintenance. Show cats require special attention and can be very rewarding for the owners willing to put in the time and effort required to win shows. Our show cats are the most expensive we offer because they are purebred from our grand champions. We hand select the best kittens suited for show, and with the required time and effort you are sure to experience many wins at your shows. Show cats are for serious cat show competitors, and well worth the money. They are just beautiful to look at.

Show cat (pure bred) $1800 and up
