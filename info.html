How it works

To purchase a kitten you first request a quote, and if you are ok with the quote, which includes shipping, shots, and paperwork, then you would make a non-refundable deposit to hold your kitten. We ship to all 50 states and guarantee safe delivery of your new pet.

About us

Our goal is to raise beautiful, healthy ragdoll kittens that are loving, playful, and well adjusted. We breed to the Ragdoll cat standards of The International Cat Association (TICA) and Cat Fanciers Association (CFA). All of our ragdoll breeders and litters of kittens are registered with TICA.

Why a Ragdoll

Choosing a Ragdoll is a simple decision, when you consider the options. If you have have ever owned another cat, you should see a difference in behavior, and you will be happy and thankful with your Ragdoll. These cats are not aggressive and may even be unaware they have nails. Ragdolls are great with everyone and even other pets.
